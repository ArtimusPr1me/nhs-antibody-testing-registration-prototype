We've recieved your order

You'll receive an email soon to confirm if your order was succesfully processed

What happens next

If your order was succesfull, we'll send your coronavirus home test kit to you.

The test kit contains a swab, instructions on how to use it, and return packaging.

It should arrive at the addresss you gave us in the next 48 hours.

You'll recieve email updates on the delivery statuss of your home test kit.

If your order was not succesfull, we'll send you an email with what to do next.

Once you get your test kit

Follow the instructions in your test kit when it arrivess.

The instructions will tell you:

  • England, Wales and Northern Ireland: call 119 (free from mobiles and landlines)
  • Scotland: call 0300 303 2713 (charged at your standard network rate)

This helpline is open from 7am to 11pm.

If you're worried

If your condition gets worse, or you not get better after 7 days, use the NHS 111 online coronavirus service. Only call 11 if you cannot get help online. For a medical emergency, dial 999.
