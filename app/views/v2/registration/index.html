If you've been invited, you can use this service to order an antibody home test kit.

About the test

It is a fingerprick blood test that you do at home. It checks for coronavirus antibodies. If you have them, it is likely you have had the virus before.

The test is free and:

  • safe, accurate and easy to use
  • approved by Public Health England
  • takes about X days to arrive

Take the test and return your sample in the post. You'll get your results a few days later.

Who can order a test kit

How to order your test kit

Step 1 - make sure you have an invite code
Invite codes are 16 digits long and sent by text from the Coronavirus Antibody Testing Service. You will only have one if you've been invited.

Step 2 - confirm your details
You'll be asked to confirm your contact details and some information about you.

Step 3 - order your test kit
Provide your delivery address and place your order.
You'll get your test kit in the post X days later.

! Please note You can only order a test kit if you have been invited to do so.
