You can only order a coronavirus antibody home test kit if you've been invited.

If you do not have an invite code, you can check you're eligible and sign up.

About the test

It is a free fingerprick blood test that you do at home. It checks for coronavirus antibodies. If you have them, it is likely you have had the virus before.

Take the test and return your sample in the post. You'll get your results a few days later.

How to order your test kit

First, enter your 16-digit invite code (it's in the text you received from GOVUK). You will only have one if you've been invited.

Then, answer some quick questions and enter your contact details to place your order. This will take 5 minutes.

You'll get your test kit in the post within 5 days.
