Testing to check if you have coronavirus now

Get an 'antigen' or 'swab' test to check if you have coronavirus (COVID-19) now.

You can get this test if:

  • you have coronavirus symptoms and are not an essential worker
  • you're and essential worker
  • you're getting tests for care home residents or staff members

Testing to check if you've had coronavirus before

Do a 'fingerprick blood test at home to find out if you've had coronavirus before.

This test helps the NHS find out how many people have had the virus. It's known as an 'antibody' test.

If you've tested positive for antibodies it does not mean you're definitely immune to coronavirus.

NHS test and trace: stopping the virus from spreading

  • test anyone with coronavirus symptoms quickly to find out if they have the virus
  • traces recent contacts of anyone who tests positive and, if necessary, tells them to self-isolate
