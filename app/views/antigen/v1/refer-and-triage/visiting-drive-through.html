About the test

  • you need to book your test slot in the first 5 days of having symptoms
  • there are both walk-through and drive-through test sites
  • site staff will tell you if an assistant does the swab test for you or if you do it yourself
  • do not go to the site if you're very unwell or if you're at high risk (shielding) from coronavirus - choose a home test instead

Who can have it

You can get a test kit for yourself and up to 3 other people you live with who have coronavirus symptoms.

In England and Wales, a child can have a test whatever their age. In Scotland and Northern Ireland, children have to be aged 5 and over.

If you visit a drive-through site

You or someone you live with must have a car or small van. Motorbikes are not suitable.

Everyone must be in the same vehicle and sit next to a window that opens.

You'll stay in your vehicle during your visit.

What you'll need to continue

  • your mobile number
  • the registration number of your vehicle, if you're going to a drive-through
  • the name and mobile number of any other people you live with who will be tested
